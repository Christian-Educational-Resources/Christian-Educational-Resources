Mission of CER

Home

The mission of CER is simply to provide Christian educational resources for free.
Too often, a quality Christian education is unavailable to believers who are at the lower end of the economic spectrum. In some cases, certain Christian educational resources have not been produced because such resources may be considered "high risk" for "low profit" by business establishments. Any believer who has searched for quality college-level Christian liberal arts textbooks is painfully aware of this unfortunate reality. When Christian educational resources have indeed been produced, the price is often set at a level which only high-middle to upperclass families can afford. To offset this, CER aspires to provide as many free resources as possible in order to put a quality private Christian education within reach of all believers. Christians are challenged by Galations 6:2 "Bear ye one another's burdens, and so fulfil the law of Christ."
