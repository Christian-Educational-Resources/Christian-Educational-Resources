Christian Educational Resources (CER)

Welcome! CER was established as a non-profit organinization with the purpose of providing free Christian educational resources. Why offer free resources?

The Bible teaches that those who receive freely from God should give freely.

Jesus tells his disciples in Matthew 10:8, "Heal the sick, cleanse the lepers, raise the dead, cast out devils: freely ye have received, freely give."

We read in 1 Corinthians 2:12, "Now we have received, not the spirit of the world, but the spirit which is of God; that we might know the things that are freely given to us of God."

The BAD News:

  • Romans 3:10  "As it is written, There is none righteous, no, not one:"
  • Romans 3:23  "For all have sinned, and come short of the glory of God;" 
  • Romans 6:23 (to be continued) "For the wages of sin is death; ..." 

The GOOD News:

  • Romans 6:23 (continued) " ... but the gift of God is eternal life through Jesus Christ our Lord."
  • Romans 10:9 "That if thou shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus, and shalt believe in thine heart that God hath raised him from the dead, thou shalt be saved." 
  • John 3:16 "For God so loved the world, that he gave his only begotten Son, that whosoever believeth in him should not perish, but have everlasting life."

There Is NO Other Way!

  • John 14:6 "Jesus saith unto him, I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father, but by me."
