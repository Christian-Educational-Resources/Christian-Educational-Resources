Christian Educational Resources (CER)

1 Corinthians 2:12  Now we have received, not the spirit of the world, but the spirit which is of God; that we might know the things that are freely given to us of God. 

Goals:

  1. To Honor Christ and Save the Lost by Sharing the Gospel Message
  2. To Promote the Establishment of New Bible-Believing Christian Educational Institutions
  3. To Provide Christian Educational Materials and Services Freely to Christian School Administrators, Teachers, and Students

    4. Mission: To further the Gospel Message of Jesus Christ by offering support to Christian Schools.
