Christian Educational Resources (CER) was founded in 2023 to offer resources to Christian parents, professional educators, and students at all levels who desire to pursue private Christian education but struggle financially to do so. CER is a non-profit organization and the resources offered through CER are free to all. The organization is set up to operate through the biblical principal of voluntary giving rather than through charging fees for products and services.
CER is operated and governed by a chief and his council consisting of from five to ten advisory council members. The chief and and council members must be professing Christians with clear evidence of sacrificial Christ-like service. This stipulation is not intended to speak animosity toward unbelievers but to foster biblical unity among believers within the organization. We are commanded in 2nd Corintians 6:14 "Be ye not unequally yoked together with unbelievers: for what fellowship hath righteousness with unrighteousness? and what communion hath light with darkness?"
